Wedding at Marywood Retreat & Conference Center — May 2026

May 2026

A beautiful venue, an incredible vendor team, and a dance floor full of celebration—Abby & Austin's wedding day was one to remember.

On May 22, 2026, Abby & Austin celebrated their beautiful wedding reception at Marywood Convention Center, surrounded by family, friends, and a talented team of wedding professionals who helped bring every detail to life. From heartfelt moments throughout the reception to an energetic dance floor that kept guests celebrating, the evening reflected the joy and excitement of their new beginning together.

Footloose Entertainment was honored to provide wedding DJ and entertainment services, with DJ Kris keeping the reception flowing seamlessly from the grand entrance and first dances to the final song of the night. Every transition, announcement, and celebration was thoughtfully coordinated to create an unforgettable experience for the newlyweds and their guests.

The day was beautifully documented by River Rae Photography, while every detail—from elegant florals and stunning bridal beauty to delicious desserts and late-night snacks—came together thanks to an outstanding team of wedding vendors. Abby & Austin's wedding at Marywood Convention Center was a wonderful reminder that the best celebrations are created through teamwork, thoughtful planning, and unforgettable moments shared with the people you love most.
