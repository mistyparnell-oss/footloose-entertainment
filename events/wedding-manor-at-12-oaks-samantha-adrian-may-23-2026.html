Wedding at Manor at 12 oaks — May 2026

May 2026

Every wedding has its own personality, and Samantha & Adrian's celebration was filled with warmth, laughter, and the kind of moments that remind us why we love what we do.

Samantha & Adrian's wedding at Manor at 12 Oaks was a beautiful celebration filled with heartfelt moments, nonstop dancing, and an incredible wedding reception. Every detail came together thanks to a talented team of wedding professionals. Laura Perez Photography captured every joyful smile, while Ben Cross Photography preserved the day's most meaningful moments on film. Elegant florals by Parkers Events, delicious desserts from Kristen's Cupcake, stylish rentals by Event Works, and flawless beauty by Nic Studios helped bring the couple's vision to life. Rob Isley kept the dance floor packed all evening, and Footloose Entertainment was honored to be part of the wedding entertainment team that helped create a fun, seamless celebration from beginning to end.
