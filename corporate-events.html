The Events

Every kind of corporate event.

From intimate 30-guest board dinners to 500-guest holiday galas, we have produced the full range of corporate event formats across Jacksonville and Northeast Florida. Each format has its own rhythm — and we know all of them.

Holiday parties.

The annual celebration that sets the tone for company culture. Festive and inclusive playlists, polished MC work, and the option to add a branded photobooth so the night lives beyond the evening.

Award ceremonies & galas.

Recognition events that demand polished MC work and precise timing — custom walk-up music for honorees, coordinated mic hand-offs, and seamless transitions between award categories.

Team building & appreciation.

Employee appreciation evenings, department celebrations, and company picnics. Energy that encourages interaction without overpowering conversation — with interactive add-ons as organic icebreakers.

Product launches.

Walk-in music, panel transitions, lapel mics for every speaker, and the option to add dramatic special effects for the product reveal. AV-first execution so technology never becomes the story.

Conferences & AGMs.

Client appreciation.
