About TPC Sawgrass

TPC Sawgrass is home to the PLAYERS Stadium Course and one of Northeast Florida's most recognized golf clubhouses, located at 110 Championship Way in Ponte Vedra Beach. The 77,000-square-foot Mediterranean Revival clubhouse features vaulted wood ceilings, iron chandeliers, terra cotta tile, and a grand staircase — a setting couples and corporate planners book specifically for its recognizable golf-course prestige.

TPC Sawgrass has been inducted into The Knot's "Best of Weddings" Hall of Fame and named Best Country Club Venue by Jacksonville Magazine's Bride Edition, alongside a steady calendar of corporate golf outings and business meetings.

Weddings & Corporate Events

TPC Sawgrass books both markets seriously rather than treating one as secondary:

For Weddings

Ceremonies on the event lawn, indoor receptions, plus rehearsal dinners, bridal showers, and a groom's golf outing on the course itself — a signature option unique to a golf clubhouse venue.

For Corporate Events

Meetings, client golf outings, and business events benefit from the same banquet spaces plus direct access to the course for a round before or after the meeting — a strong option for client entertainment.

Planning Your Entertainment

Whether you're planning a wedding reception or a corporate event, TPC Sawgrass's mix of an open-air event lawn and indoor banquet rooms means your DJ or entertainment provider needs a setup plan matched to your specific space — outdoor lawn ceremonies and indoor clubhouse receptions have different sound coverage and power needs.

What we'd confirm before your event: whether you're using the event lawn, an indoor banquet room, or both; load-in access and timing coordinated with the club's event staff; and power availability for outdoor lawn setups.

As a Northeast Florida DJ and event entertainment company, we plan the setup around whichever TPC Sawgrass space you've booked, for weddings and corporate events alike.
