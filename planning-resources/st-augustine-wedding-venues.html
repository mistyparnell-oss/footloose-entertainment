Venue Roundup · St. Augustine, FL

Best St. Augustine Wedding Venues

A roundup of the venues couples ask us about most, from historic downtown buildings to golf clubhouses along the coast.

St. Augustine draws couples for a reason — few cities in Florida combine historic architecture, waterfront views, and walkable downtown charm the way America's oldest city does. Here are eight venues that come up constantly when couples ask us for recommendations, with what to know about each.

01

The White Room

St. Augustine's only private waterfront venue in the historic district, built in 1888, with three distinct spaces — the Grand Ballroom, The Loft & Rooftop, and the more intimate Villa Blanca. Consistently one of the most awarded venues on The Knot and WeddingWire.

Read our full White Room guide →
02

Treasury on the Plaza

A converted 1920s bank building in the heart of downtown, known for The Vault — the original bank vault, now a striking bar and photo backdrop. Books exclusive-use weddings of 40–175 guests only.

Read our full Treasury on the Plaza guide →
03

Casa Monica Resort

A landmark Moorish Revival hotel in downtown St. Augustine, offering grand indoor ballroom space alongside full-service resort amenities — a strong choice for couples who want a historic feel with hotel-level catering and accommodations in one building.

04

The Legacy at World Golf Village

A ballroom-style venue in World Golf Village offering polished, full-service wedding packages away from the downtown historic district — a good fit for couples who want a more traditional ballroom feel with easier parking and access.

05

The Carriage House

A rustic-elegant venue with flexible indoor/outdoor space, including garden ceremony options for couples who want greenery and natural light rather than a downtown historic building.

06

Crystal Ballroom St. Augustine

A ballroom venue known for large windows and abundant natural light, giving receptions a bright, airy feel that photographs differently than the darker, more historic downtown spaces.

07

The Magnolia Room at Ponce de Leon's Fountain of Youth

A waterfront venue set within one of St. Augustine's most historically significant properties — a distinctive option for couples who want their wedding tied to the city's founding history.

08

The Palencia Club

A 33,000-square-foot Mediterranean-style clubhouse accommodating up to 180 guests for a cocktail reception or 150 for seated dining and dancing — a golf-club setting a bit outside the downtown core.

Planning entertainment across these venues: downtown historic buildings like The White Room and Treasury on the Plaza have fixed architecture and specific load-in routes, while golf clubhouse venues like The Legacy and The Palencia Club tend to have more flexible, purpose-built event space. As a Jacksonville and St. Augustine wedding DJ company, we plan sound and setup around whichever venue you book rather than a one-size-fits-all approach.
