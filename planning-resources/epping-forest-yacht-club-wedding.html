About Epping Forest Yacht & Country Club

Epping Forest Yacht & Country Club sits on the banks of the St. Johns River at 1830 Epping Forest Drive in Jacksonville, built around the historic duPont Mansion. The property pairs the mansion's period architecture with formal English Gardens and river views, and the club can accommodate everything from an intimate dinner for 10 to a large celebration of several hundred guests.

The combination of indoor mansion rooms and outdoor garden and terrace space is what sets Epping Forest apart from most Jacksonville venues — couples can host a garden ceremony, cocktail hour on the terrace, and a reception inside the mansion, all on the same property.

Room & Space Options

Epping Forest offers several distinct spaces, so the right one for your wedding depends mainly on your guest count and whether you want an indoor or outdoor feel:

If you're planning a smaller wedding, the Forest or Gate Room keeps things intimate. Larger weddings typically use the Dupont & Mason Room or the full second floor indoors, with the English Gardens as the go-to for a large outdoor ceremony or reception.

Why Couples Choose Epping Forest

  • Riverfront setting: Panoramic St. Johns River views from the terraces and gardens.
  • Historic architecture: The mansion's period details give indoor receptions a distinct look that's hard to replicate at a hotel ballroom.
  • Indoor/outdoor flexibility: Ceremony in the gardens, cocktail hour on the terrace, reception inside — all without changing venues.
  • On-site event coordination: The club provides a dedicated wedding coordinator to help with room selection, catering, and day-of logistics.

Planning Your Entertainment

Footloose Entertainment works weddings at Epping Forest Yacht & Country Club regularly, so we already know the rooms, the load-in routes, and the venue's coordination team — not something we're figuring out for the first time on your wedding day.

Because Epping Forest spans both historic indoor rooms and open-air garden and terrace spaces, your entertainment setup needs to match whichever space you book. An outdoor garden ceremony has different sound and power needs than a reception inside the Dupont & Mason Room, and we plan for the specific space you've booked rather than showing up with a one-size-fits-all setup.

What we already know from working this venue: the load-in routes for each room, how sound carries across the gardens and terraces versus indoors, power access points for outdoor setups, and how to coordinate directly with Epping Forest's event staff on timing.

If you're booking Epping Forest, that familiarity means fewer unknowns on your wedding day — we're not learning the venue at the same time we're running your reception.
