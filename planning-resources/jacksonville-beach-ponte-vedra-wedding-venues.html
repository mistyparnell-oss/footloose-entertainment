Jacksonville Beach and Ponte Vedra Beach form Northeast Florida's most concentrated stretch of oceanfront and coastal wedding venues — rooftop event spaces overlooking the Atlantic, historic beachfront hotels, and resort clubs with decades of wedding history. As a wedding DJ company that works this coastline regularly, here are eleven venues that come up most often when couples ask us for recommendations, five right in Jacksonville Beach and six along the Ponte Vedra corridor just south of it.

Jacksonville Beach Venues

01 Jacksonville Beach

Private 131

A modern rooftop-style event space in the heart of Jacksonville Beach overlooking Latham Park and the ocean, with a 2,100-square-foot indoor ballroom and 1,600 square feet of outdoor balconies. Seats up to 130 guests indoors, or accommodates up to 300 using both indoor and outdoor space together. Pricing generally runs $3,500–$9,500 depending on day and time.

private131.com

02 Jacksonville Beach

Casa Marina Hotel & Restaurant

A historic 1925 boutique hotel directly on the Jacksonville Beach oceanfront, blending vintage architecture with a relaxed coastal feel. Offers both an indoor ballroom and outdoor beachfront ceremony space, with capacity ranging up to the low 200s depending on the specific space and setup.

casamarinahotel.com

03 Jacksonville Beach

One Ocean Resort (rebranding to Dune House in 2026)

An oceanfront resort in nearby Atlantic Beach offering several distinct event spaces — an Oceanfront Courtyard, an indoor Ballroom, and smaller rooms for more intimate gatherings — scaling from elopements up to receptions of around 250 guests. Note that this property is in the process of rebranding from One Ocean Resort to Dune House, so you may see both names in use through the transition.

oneoceanresort.com

04 Jacksonville Beach

Margaritaville Jacksonville Beach Hotel

A beachfront hotel offering an open-air oceanview Terrace for ceremonies of up to 100 guests, plus the indoor Seagrass Meeting Room (up to 118) for a reception or rehearsal dinner — both spaces can be booked together for a full ceremony-and-reception day.

margaritavilleresorts.com

05 Jacksonville Beach Area

Hilton Jacksonville at Mayo Clinic

A full-service hotel near the Beaches with over 21,000 square feet of flexible event space, including the NorthStar Ballroom (up to 1,600 guests) and an outdoor Event Lawn accommodating up to 400 — the highest-capacity venue in this roundup, with 252 on-site guest rooms for out-of-town wedding parties.

hilton.com

08 Ponte Vedra

Ponte Vedra Inn & Club

A longstanding oceanfront resort with over 25,000 square feet of event space across multiple rooms and lawns. The Ponte Vedra Ballroom alone can host up to 500 guests standing or 350 for a plated dinner, with additional options like the Oceanfront Courtyard (up to 200 seated) and West Lawn (up to 350) — the most scalable venue in this roundup.

pontevedra.com

09 Ponte Vedra

The Lodge & Club

An oceanfront sister property to Ponte Vedra Inn & Club with over 13,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor event space. Beachfront ceremony space accommodates up to 200 guests, with an indoor Ocean Room available as a weather backup for larger ceremonies.

pontevedra.com

10 Ponte Vedra

The Plantation at Ponte Vedra

A private golf and beach club specializing in more intimate affairs, accommodating up to 130 guests — a good fit for couples who want a country-club setting without a large guest list.

theplantationpvb.com

11 Ponte Vedra

Marsh Landing Country Club

A country club venue with a Ballroom seating up to 250 for larger weddings, plus smaller spaces like the Sitting Room and Dining Room for more intimate events, and a covered Pavilion accommodating up to 120 guests for an outdoor ceremony.

marshlandingevents.com

Choosing Among These Venues

With eleven venues spanning downtown Jacksonville Beach to the resort corridor of Ponte Vedra, the right fit usually comes down to guest count, how much you want the ocean visually present in your ceremony, and whether you're drawn to a modern event space or a longer-established resort.

For genuinely oceanfront ceremonies, Casa Marina, One Ocean Resort (Dune House), Margaritaville Jacksonville Beach, Ponte Vedra Inn & Club, and The Lodge & Club all put you directly on or steps from the sand. Private 131 trades beachfront for a more modern, design-forward rooftop-style space a short walk or drive from the water, with ocean views from its balcony. The Hilton Jacksonville at Mayo Clinic sits a bit further inland near the Beaches area, trading direct oceanfront for a much larger, full-service hotel footprint.

Guest count varies enormously across this list. The Hilton Jacksonville at Mayo Clinic's 1,600-guest NorthStar Ballroom is the largest capacity by far, with Ponte Vedra Inn & Club's 500-guest Ballroom and Marsh Landing's 250-guest Ballroom also strong choices for larger weddings, while The Plantation at Ponte Vedra's 130-guest cap suits smaller, more intimate weddings. TPC Sawgrass and Sawgrass Marriott sit in between, with the added benefit of being well-suited to combined wedding-and-rehearsal-dinner or wedding-and-corporate-event bookings — see our dedicated guides for both for exact numbers.

Booking timelines along this coast run on the same rhythm as the rest of Northeast Florida — peak wedding season (roughly October through April) books out well over a year in advance at the more established resort venues like Ponte Vedra Inn & Club, so it's worth reaching out early if you have a specific Saturday in mind.

Planning entertainment across these venues: beachfront and rooftop spaces like Private 131, Casa Marina, and the Ponte Vedra resort lawns typically mean outdoor ceremony sound plus a separate indoor reception setup, with wind and ocean noise to plan around.
