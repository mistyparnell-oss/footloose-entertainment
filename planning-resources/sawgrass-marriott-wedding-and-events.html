About Sawgrass Marriott Golf Resort & Spa

Sawgrass Marriott Golf Resort & Spa sits at 1000 Tournament Players Club Blvd in Ponte Vedra Beach, with over 90,000 square feet of flexible indoor and outdoor event space. As a full-scale Marriott resort with golf, spa, and oceanfront access, it's built to handle everything from a large wedding to a multi-day corporate conference.

The resort's scale is its main differentiator from smaller boutique venues — multiple ballrooms operating simultaneously, on-site accommodations for out-of-town guests, and full catering and event planning support in-house.

Weddings & Corporate Events

For Weddings

Oceanfront ceremonies at the Cabana Beach Club, waterfall-framed outdoor space at the Cascade Lawn, and grand ballroom receptions with full on-site catering and accommodations for guests.

For Corporate Events

The resort is a genuine conference destination — large-capacity ballrooms, breakout meeting space, and golf and spa amenities make it a common choice for multi-day corporate events and client retreats.

Planning Your Entertainment

Because Sawgrass Marriott operates multiple ballrooms and outdoor venues simultaneously, your DJ needs to confirm the exact space booked well in advance — sound, power, and load-in logistics differ significantly between an oceanfront ceremony at the Cabana Beach Club and an indoor reception in the Arbor Ballroom.

What we'd confirm before your event: which ballroom or outdoor space you've booked, load-in routes and timing through the resort's catering and events team, and power access for any oceanfront or lawn setup.

As a Northeast Florida DJ and event entertainment company, we coordinate directly with the resort's event staff on these details for both weddings and corporate events.
