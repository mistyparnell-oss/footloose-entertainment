The Plan

How we plan a corporate event before doors open.

A polished corporate event is built in the weeks before, not on the night of. Once you book, we work alongside your event coordinator to lock down the run-of-show, music direction, vendor handoffs, and on-site logistics — so when guests arrive, every cue is already in place.

01

Confirm the run-of-show.

We start with a working session against your event timeline — doors, welcome, dinner service, program, awards, toasts, dancing, and close. Every transition gets a music cue and a clear handoff between speakers and the DJ booth.

02

Lock down music direction.

You tell us the energy you want at each phase, the songs or genres that are off the table, and any specific tracks tied to your company history or program. We draft custom direction and you approve it before the event.

03

Coordinate with your team.

We reach out to your venue contact, caterer, AV team, and event planner to align on load-in times, power, sightlines, and program flow. By the day of the event, every vendor knows what we are doing and when.

04

Show up ready to execute.

We arrive early for setup, run a sound check with your speakers and any presenters, and confirm last-minute changes with your coordinator. By the time the first guest walks in, everything is dialed in and quietly running.

The Event

Your corporate evening, from doors to dancing.

A corporate event has its own rhythm — cocktails build, dinner settles, the program tightens, and dancing or networking carries it home. Across 1,000+ events in Jacksonville and Northeast Florida, we know the cues that make every phase land.

01

Doors & Cocktails

Music starts the moment doors open. Sophisticated instrumentals at conversation volume so guests can mingle, network, and find their seats without competing with the playlist.

02

Welcome & Seating

Your MC delivers a warm welcome, calls guests to their tables, and hands the room to the speakers. Smooth, on time, and never awkward.

03

Dinner Service

Background music tuned to the room while plates move. We coordinate with your caterer so course transitions and music levels match.

04

Toasts & Speeches

Wireless mics ready for every speaker. Music ducks under remarks, lifts cleanly between, and the timing stays tight to your program.

05

Awards & Program

Custom walk-up music for honorees, coordinated mic hand-offs to recipients, and on-the-mark transitions between award categories.

06

Energy Shift

Once the formal program ends, we read the room and lift the energy — signaling guests that the evening is shifting from program to celebration.

07

Open Dancing

Crowd-pleasers across decades, mixed live to keep the floor full — or held at a polished background level if your event stays seated for networking.

08

Final Toast & Send-Off

A clean wind-down with your closing remarks, a final song that matches the night, and a tidy breakdown that respects your venue’s clock.
