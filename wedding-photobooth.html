The Output

Printed, Digital & Video — Your Wedding, Your Way

Every photo booth experience ends with something to take home. What that looks like depends on the booth you choose — from instant printed keepsakes to shareable digital moments to cinematic video memories. Here’s what’s possible.

Printed

Instant on-site prints in multiple formats — 2×6 strips, 4×6, 5×7, and panoramic 4×24 prints available depending on your booth. Every print is branded with your custom wedding overlay. Available on standard, metallic, glitter, and transparent paper stocks. Open air, mirror, enclosed, and wooden booths.

Digital

Photos, boomerangs, GIFs, and AI-generated images delivered instantly to guests’ phones via QR code or text — no app required. Every image is collected in an online gallery you can access, download, and share after the wedding. Available with all booth types.

Video

Slow-motion glamour reels from the 360 booth and cinematic Glambot footage — all delivered digitally and compiled into your online gallery. The video options that make your wedding feel like a film. Available with select booth types.

Output options vary by booth. Full details — including print formats, paper stocks, and keepsake add-ons — are available on each individual booth page.
