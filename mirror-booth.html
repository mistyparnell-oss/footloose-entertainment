Full-Length Interactive Mirror Booth

Elegant Decor. Instant Photos. Endless Fun.

The mirror photo booth is a sleek, full-length touchscreen mirror that guides guests through a fun, animated photo experience. On-screen prompts, countdowns, and playful animations make it intuitive and exciting for guests of every age. The result is a stunning photo — with your custom event overlay — delivered instantly as a print, digitally to their phone, or both. It’s the perfect blend of sophistication and entertainment that fits seamlessly into any event aesthetic, from elegant weddings to high-energy corporate parties to unforgettable proms.

Mirror Photo Booth FAQs

Everything you need to know before booking

A mirror photo booth is a full-length interactive touchscreen mirror that photographs guests using animated on-screen prompts and countdowns. It applies a custom overlay for your event and delivers photos instantly as prints, digitally to guests’ phones, or both. It’s a beautiful piece of event decor that also doubles as the most entertaining experience in the room.

Yes! Guests can receive instant printed photos on the spot, digital copies via QR code or text, or both — it’s completely customizable to your event. Prints make great party favors and guest book additions. Digital delivery means guests can share immediately to social media from the event.

Absolutely. Every booking includes a fully custom overlay designed for your specific event — your names and wedding date, company logo and brand colors, school name and prom theme, or any design you have in mind. We create a proof and share it for your approval before the event day.

The mirror itself requires about 4x6 feet of floor space. We recommend a clear area of roughly 8x8 feet so guests can step back comfortably for full-length photos. We coordinate with your venue coordinator in advance to identify the perfect placement.

Yes — props are included with every mirror booth rental. Our attendant sets up a fun prop station and helps guests choose items that match the vibe of your event. Props add an extra element of fun and make for more memorable, shareable photos.

Yes! We serve Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra Beach, Orange Park, Fleming Island, Jacksonville Beach, Fernandina Beach, and all of Northeast Florida. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific venue and date.

Absolutely — combining the mirror booth with our DJ services, 360 video booth, or other photobooth options creates a complete entertainment experience, often at better combined value. Contact us to discuss options for your event.

We recommend booking as early as possible—popular Saturday dates during wedding season and holiday corporate event season can fill up 3 to 6 months in advance. Contact us as soon as your date is confirmed to check availability.

Our team arrives 1 hour before your event to set up, test, and ensure everything is running perfectly before your first guests arrive. We coordinate with your venue in advance to confirm load-in access and placement.

The mirror booth can be used outdoors with proper shelter, a level surface, and access to a standard power outlet. Northeast Florida’s heat and humidity require a covered or climate-controlled area to protect the touchscreen components. We’ll discuss your specific outdoor venue during the planning process to confirm feasibility.
