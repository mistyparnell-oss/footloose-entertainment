The Booth

Eight booth options. One that fits your event.

A corporate photobooth has a different job than a wedding one. It is a brand asset, a content engine for your marketing channels, an icebreaker between teams that rarely meet in person, and a conversation piece for clients you want to remember the night. Here is the full range — every option includes a custom-branded overlay.

Open Air Photobooth

Versatile · Most Popular

The reliable workhorse. Large groups fit comfortably, custom-branded prints come out instantly.

  • Instant branded prints
  • Custom company overlay
  • Large group friendly
  • On-site attendant included

A spinning arm captures slow-motion 360° video of your guests, branded with your logo, hashtag, and product imagery. The content guests share to LinkedIn and Instagram all night — the booth most often booked for product launches and high-profile galas.

Digital Photobooth

Paperless · Lead-Capture Ready

All-digital photos, boomerangs, and GIFs delivered to guests via QR code or text. Optional email and consent capture turns the booth into a low-friction lead-gen tool for conferences and trade shows — while staying paperless.

A handcrafted wooden booth that reads as decor as much as it does entertainment. The natural finish disappears tastefully into upscale venues — the right choice for buttoned-up dinners, donor appreciation events, and executive receptions.

Enclosed Photobooth

Private · Approachable

Step inside, close the curtain, and loosen up. The privacy of the enclosed format draws in guests who would never approach an open-air booth — perfect for employee appreciation parties where engagement is the metric that matters.

The Output

Branded prints, social-ready digital, cinematic video.

Every booth produces something your guests take home — and something your marketing team can reuse. Here is the full output picture, from physical keepsakes to content that lives on your company channels long after the event.

Printed

Instant on-site prints in multiple formats — 2×6 strips, 4×6, 5×7, and panoramic 4×24 prints. Every print is branded with your company overlay. Available on standard, metallic, glitter, and transparent paper. Pairs with open-air, mirror, enclosed, and wooden booths.

The Flow

From booking to last photo.

Setting up a corporate photobooth should not become another open ticket on your event planner’s list. Here is exactly how the work happens between booking and breakdown.

01

Brand assets & overlay design.

Once your date is locked, we collect your logo, brand guidelines, event name, hashtag, and any product imagery. Our team designs a custom overlay matched to your visual identity and sends a proof for approval — usually 2–3 weeks before the event.

02

Early load-in & setup.

We arrive at your venue an hour before your photobooth time starts, coordinate with your event planner and venue contact, and have the booth tested and styled before the first guest arrives. Backup gear stays on hand the entire event.

03

Attendant available to run the booth.

Most events include a professional attendant who stays with the booth through your entire event — keeping energy up, helping guests through the flow, encouraging participation, and making sure every output is on-brand.

One vendor. Zero coordination overhead. When you book your DJ, photobooth, or special effects through Footloose, every team is coordinated by the same company — setup, timing, and transitions all handled together. Your event planner runs the event, not the vendors.

The Experiences & Keepsakes

Elevate the booth. Send guests home with something.

Beyond the booth itself, these are the creative activations and branded keepsakes that turn a corporate photobooth into something memorable — and something that lives on desks, in offices, and on social feeds long after the event ends.

Photo booth experiences

Creative activations that turn the booth into a conversation piece — not just another vendor station.

AI Photobooth

Watercolor, sketches, and themed transformations turn every guest into custom artwork — perfect for retreats, sales kickoffs, or culture-driven events.

Glam Booth

Soft-focus studio lighting and high-fashion filters give every guest a polished, magazine-quality portrait. The natural fit for award galas and executive recognition.

Mosaic

Guest photos assemble in real time into one large composite image — your logo, a product, or a team portrait. A wall-ready piece of content built from your guests.

Formal Portrait Station

Professional studio lighting and a dedicated backdrop give every guest a polished portrait — perfect for headshot refreshes or executive recognition events.

Flower Walls

A lush floral backdrop that doubles as decor — works as a booth backdrop or standalone selfie station and photographs beautifully for your social channels.

Branded keepsakes & favors

Tangible takeaways guests keep on their desks and in their offices — everyday reminders of your event and your brand.

Keychains

A small acrylic keychain with the guest’s photo embedded inside, branded with your company mark. Practical, durable, and reaches every guest’s daily carry.

Magnets

Custom photo magnets guests put on their fridge or office filing cabinet. One of the most enduring corporate favors — still visible months and years later.

Bookmarks

Elegant bookmarks printed with the guest’s photo and your event branding. A refined, practical takeaway — particularly resonant for book-club, mentorship, or board recognition contexts.

Viewfinders

Retro viewfinder toys loaded with the guest’s photo. Nostalgic, tactile, and completely unlike any other corporate favor — the kind of thing that ends up on a desk for years.

Premium Paper Stocks

Upgrade your prints to glitter, metallic, or transparent paper. The right choice for galas, awards events, and brand activations where the takeaway should feel premium.
