360 Booth FAQs

Everything you need to know before booking

A 360 video booth features a circular platform guests stand on while a rotating arm camera sweeps 360 degrees around them, capturing glamorous slow-motion video from every angle. The result is a cinematic, shareable video clip with a custom overlay that guests receive instantly on their phones. It works beautifully for weddings, corporate events, school events, and private parties.

The platform is approximately 4 feet in diameter and the rotating arm extends outward, so we recommend a clear footprint of roughly 10×10 feet. We coordinate directly with your venue coordinator before your event to confirm placement and ensure a smooth setup.

Our platform comfortably holds 2–6 guests per session, making it perfect for couples, groups, teams, and families. Our attendant manages the flow to keep energy high and ensure everyone gets their moment—often multiple times!

Absolutely! Every video features a fully custom digital overlay designed specifically for your event. For weddings that’s your names and date. For corporate events it’s your logo and branding. For school events it’s your school name and theme. We create a proof before your event for approval.

Guests receive their personalized video instantly via QR code or text message. Videos are ready to share directly to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, or any other platform within seconds of their spin. Every video is also saved to your private online gallery.

Yes! We serve Jacksonville, St. Augustine, Ponte Vedra Beach, Jacksonville Beach, Neptune Beach, Atlantic Beach, Orange Park, Fleming Island, Fernandina Beach, and all of Northeast Florida. Contact us to confirm travel availability for your specific venue location.

Absolutely—and many clients do exactly that! Combining our 360 booth with DJ services or other photobooth options creates a full entertainment package at often a better combined value. Contact us to discuss bundling options for your event.

We recommend booking as early as possible—especially for peak seasons like spring (prom and wedding season) and the holiday corporate event season in November and December. Popular Saturday dates can book out 6–12 months in advance. That said, we do occasionally have last-minute availability, so it never hurts to reach out even for upcoming events.

Our team typically arrives 1.5–2 hours before your event start time to set up, test, and calibrate the booth. We coordinate directly with your venue to confirm load-in access and ensure everything is ready and running well before your first guests arrive—so your event starts flawlessly.

Your private online gallery is available for an extended period following your event so you and your guests have plenty of time to download, share, and relive every video. We’ll provide gallery access details as part of your event wrap-up communication.

The 360 booth requires a standard 110V electrical outlet within approximately 50 feet of the setup location. We prefer a dedicated circuit to prevent any interruptions during peak use, but most event venues accommodate this easily. Our team performs a site check before the event to confirm all technical requirements are met.

The 360 booth can be used for outdoor events in favorable weather conditions, provided there is a covered or sheltered area, a level surface, and reliable power access. Northeast Florida’s climate means we take extra precautions for heat, humidity, and the possibility of afternoon showers. Our team will discuss your specific outdoor setup during the planning process to ensure the best possible experience.
